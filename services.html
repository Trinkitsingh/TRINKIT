Our Services

We provide a wide range of tech services to help with whatever you need. Whether it’s fixing or troubleshooting a Chromebook, building a custom PC, or solving any other tech problems, we’re here to assist you. We can also recommend the right parts for your PC build based on your budget. From fixing software bugs and improving performance to repairing hardware and solving network issues, we can handle a variety of tech problems. Just let us know what the issue is, and we’ll work to get everything running smoothly. Our prices start at $10 for troubleshooting and $30 for custom PC builds (Note: prices may vary depending on the PC build). For other issues, we can discuss the price based on the work required.
