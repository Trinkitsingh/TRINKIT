About Us

At Trinkit, we’re all about helping you get things working again, whether it’s a broken appliance or a device that’s giving you trouble. We’ve been in the repair business for years and know how frustrating it can be when something stops working, which is why we focus on quick, reliable fixes at a fair price. Our team is skilled and friendly, and we take pride in getting your repairs done right the first time. Most of all, we believe in being honest and upfront, making sure you feel confident in the service you’re getting. When you need something repaired, we’ve got your back!
